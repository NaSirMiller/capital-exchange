# **Capital Region Community Stock Exchange**

**A local marketplace where residents can invest in small businesses in the Capital Region, creating a community-driven ecosystem of capital, growth, and engagement.**

---

## **Concept**

Many small businesses struggle to access capital, while residents want structured ways to support local businesses. This platform provides a **regional stock exchange** where:

* Businesses can issue **fractional ownership shares** based on their financial projections.
* Residents can **buy, sell, and trade shares** of local businesses.
* Businesses commit to distributing a portion of profits as **dividends** to investors.
* Optional integration with stablecoins enables **fast, low-fee transactions**.

---

## **Key Features**

1. **Business Onboarding**

   * Businesses submit verified **P&L / financial projection sheets**
   * Platform calculates **valuation, share price, and dividend commitment**
   * Shares are issued and listed for investors

2. **Investor Dashboard**

   * Browse local businesses and invest in shares
   * Track portfolio, share value, and dividends earned

3. **Marketplace & Trading**

   * Buy, sell, and trade shares in a simulated **secondary market**
   * Platform enforces dividend rules based on business profits

4. **Community Engagement**

   * Leaderboards and reputation points for active investors
   * Gamified elements to encourage participation
---
## **Impact**

* Unlock local capital for small businesses
* Engage residents as investors in their community
* Boost economic activity in the Capital Region
* Transparent, scalable, and community-driven
